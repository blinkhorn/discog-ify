Discog-ify

Easily create Spotify playlists from artist and record label discographies

Please log in using your Spotify account to export artist or record label discographies to a Spotify playlist

Description

Dicog-ify allows you to select an artist or record label and generate a Spotify playlist of their available discography once logged in to your Spotify account. For example, to generate a playlist of Robert Hood's discography, log in to your Spotify using the button above, search for, select, and submit Robert Hood on the next page, and enjoy your the playlist that will be automatically added to your Spotify playlists.

Technology Used

Discog-ify is a front-end application developed in JavaScript with jQuery and Bootstrap. Discog-ify accesses discogs.com and Spotify's APIs to generate artist and record label playlists for the user. A similar application, DiscogsToSpotify, helped give me the idea for Discog-ify by demonstrating to me that is was possible to use Discogs and Spotify's APIs in this manner. Instead of building playlists off of a user's Discog's collection, Discog-ify users can search for artists and record labels to build Spotify playlists off of without a Discogs account.
